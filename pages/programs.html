Our Programs

Community-driven initiatives preserving and sharing Chewa heritage through education, documentation, and cultural engagement.

Schools Outreach

Cultural Education

Bringing Chewa language and cultural education to schools across Malawi's Central Region. Our programs help students connect with their heritage while excelling in modern education.

Program Highlights

  • • Chichewa language workshops
  • • Cultural storytelling sessions
  • • Traditional craft demonstrations
  • • Teacher training programs
$150 sponsors one school visit Support Education

Cultural Documentation

Preserving Heritage

Recording oral histories, traditional practices, and ceremonies with community consent. Our respectful approach ensures sacred traditions are preserved appropriately.

Documentation Focus

  • • Elder interview sessions
  • • Traditional craft techniques
  • • Community ceremony recordings
  • • Digital archive development
$300 funds documentation project Fund Documentation

Community Workshops

Hands-on Learning

Hands-on learning experiences in traditional crafts, storytelling, and cultural practices. Led by master craftsmen and cultural knowledge keepers.

Workshop Types

  • • Nyau mask carving sessions
  • • Traditional pottery making
  • • Storytelling and proverbs
  • • Cultural music and dance
$75 sponsors one workshop Support Workshops

Youth Engagement

Future Guardians

Connecting young Chewa people with their heritage through mentorship, cultural activities, and leadership development programs that bridge traditional and modern worlds.

Youth Initiatives

  • • Cultural mentorship programs
  • • Youth leadership development
  • • Heritage ambassador training
  • • Inter-generational dialogue
$200 supports youth program Invest in Youth
